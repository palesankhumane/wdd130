Overview

Purpose

Based in the beautiful prairies of Cody, WY, Villa la Fleur serves couples from everywhere. We pride ourselves as a destination wedding f sorts, without the pricetag. We provide a comprehensive wedding planning service - all things from invites to cakes, wedding receptions to photography to day-after brunches, and everything in between!. You choose how involved you want us to be, and we've got you covered. Our Villa la Fleur team loves to plan create. Whether they're crafting something as specific as a floor plan or as broad as a wedding theme, we are enthusiastic and edgy. Above all else, we strive to make your celebration feel unique to you. Guided by your personal style, we will ensure your wedding is authentic and unlike anything you've ever seen! Villa la Fleur offers planning packages than come with everything from wedding day management to full-service planning to make all your wedding dreams come true!

Audience

If you're headed for "I do", then we're what your drems are made of. We're a one-stop shop to turn your dream into reality. Villa la Fleur can take the heat off as you head toward the most important day of your life (to date)!

Normal paragraph example

Villa la Fleur Nestled among cottonwood trees and with a backdrop of the majestic Rockies... Villa la Fleur provides an incomparable locality. Traveling along the winding driveway, lined by majestic live oaks, you and your guests will be transported to a place that seems to come straight out of a storybook. Once past the gatehouse, the grounds and Georgian-style mansion create an awe-inspiring first impression. rounding the mansion, you will be transported into a fairytale upon seeing the majesty of the repurposed and refurbished barn where our events are held. Situated on 155 acres of meticulously groomed grounds surrounded bu lush prairie all around, this ranch features winding creeks, tree covered paths, and beautiful lush gardens... making for an unforgettable and truly unique experience. As you step through doors at Villa la Fleur, expect unwavering hospitality, unbelievable comfort, and a beautiful place to call your own, even if just for a day. Whatever you are celebrating, we hope you know and experience the magic of our welcome. You may come in strangers, but you will leave as a friend.

Colored paragraph example

The Barn Villa la Fleur provides an unforgettable barn experience in a timeless setting complete with old world rustic charm and a touch of modern sophistication. The Barn features inlaid barn wood with reclaimed flooring. Guest will marvel when they see the custom bar designed to highlight the natural elements and subtle splendor of reclaimed wood. Chandeliers adorn the soaring 35 foot ceilings creating majestic ambiance for your special day. Every detail of the custom design and construction of the Villa la Fleur Barn will ensure your day is one not to be forgotten. Crafted from heavy timber, the Barn is adorned with exposed rafters and includes a heavy timber staircase that descends from the bridal suite. The barn will naturally enrich the anticipation for your long awaited entrance. You will be escorted through carefully curated barn doors and led toward the picturesque view overlooking the magnificent Rockies. This scene will serve as the backdrop to your wedding ceremony. The Main Barn can accommodate up to 200 seated guest in the main hall and an additional 75 guest in the lounge, providing ample space for you and your guest to dance to your hearts contentment. Every amenity has been provided to ensure that you and your guest have the experience of a lifetime. Rest rooms are conveniently located off the main hall and were designed using restored tin and forged metal from a traditional barn roof. The fully air conditioned space has three 10 foot ceiling fans to make sure everyone is comfortable in the summer. A coat closet and a fireplace in the lounge will help guest forget the winter weather and bask in the moment of your forever. The Barn is fully equipped with audiovisual support to allow for the personalization of every detail. Surround sound speakers have been installed to ensure that your guest enjoy the musical selections and the ceremony no matter the location. Television screens are available in the main hall and lounge areas so that guest can view your video and photo montages throughout the event. The Gardens A perfect location for the outdoor ceremony of your dreams, our gardens offer privacy and seclusion in a space designated to celebrate your big day. With the Rockies peaking in the background, the gardens offer tranquility and peace, permitting you to embrace the beauty of nature as you pledge your love to one another under the custom pergola.
